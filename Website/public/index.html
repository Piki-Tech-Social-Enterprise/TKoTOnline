Te Kahu o Taonui

Formed in 2006/7, the purpose of Te Kāhu o Taonui was to create a taumata for our Taitokerau Iwi Chairs to come together, to wānanga, share ideas and concerns with each other. To utilise the power of our collective Iwi to create more opportunities to benefit all of our whānau, hapū and Marae. In the COVID-19 environment, Te Kahu o Taonui established a virtual back office to centralise the management and oversight of the agreed priority areas within the rohe. This centralisation of the resource will ensure effective use of resources to support activity on the ground and to ensure resources are equitably applied across the Iwi network.
